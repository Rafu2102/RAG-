課程教學目標：
1. Develop students' understanding of the world's cultures.
2. Students will be able to discuss each topic in group and present their viewpoints to the class.
3. Develop students' critical reading and thinking skills.
4. Students will enhance their abilities in reading, grammar, vocabulary, listening and speaking in this class. Basic writing skills will also be involved in this class.

課程教學綱要：
1. For each unit, there will be: a) preview, in-class, and review activities, b) getting-reading activities to familiarize the students with each topic, b) vocabulary and structure explanation to ensure students' reading fluency, c) activities to develop students critical thinking in group, d) assignments for each unit, and e) TOEIC Test Bank.
