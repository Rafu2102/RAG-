課程教學目標：
1. Students will learn about business conversation, readings and writing in English to gain competitive advantages in a globalized business world. The objective of this course is to help students familiarize with English often used in the business world, esp. International Trade. Intercultural knowledge will also be introduced. Basically, this is a project-based course, in which students in a group will have a lot practice and make presentations.

課程教學綱要：
1. Business Conversation, readings and Letter Writing in English: how to introduce oneself, the company, the layout of business letters, e-mail and fax; Looking for Trade Partners; Making inquiries, Offers and Counter Offers, Placing Orders, Settlement, Claim Letters, Circular Letters, Product Promotion, Letters of Application, Resume

3. 上課需準時出席，上課時需將手機關機。在上課時間內使用手機上網或講電話、傳簡訊等行為一經發現，平時成績（平均分）即扣一分。
