課程教學目標：
1. This course offers a thorough, yet practical overview of language-teaching strategies and methodology for newer teachers seeking background and guidance, and for experienced professionals looking for refreshing new ideas. The book provides an overall introduction to key aspects of language-teaching methodology in an accessible, non-trivial way.

課程教學綱要：
1. This module will allow students to:
- further develop an understanding of the theoretical perspectives which underpin English language teaching and the language-learning process
- gain further practical knowledge of the teaching of English as a foreign language
- continue to gain competence in effective planning for English language teaching
- further develop an understanding of the context of English language teaching
- continue to develop a capacity for life-long professional self-development through critical reflection
- explain further aspects of the main theories of language and language teaching
- demonstrate an understanding of strategies to teach grammar
- demonstrate an understanding of strategies to teach the productive skills and incorporate appropriately a balanced and integrated approach to the teaching of linguistic skills
- demonstrate in contextualized lesson-planning an understanding of the importance of integrating a variety of technologies into the development of linguistic competence
- evaluate and integrate a variety of teaching resources into effective lesson plans
- respond at an appropriate professional level to issues relating to English language teaching and learning
- demonstrate a high level of awareness of the need for continuing profession
