課程教學目標：
1. This class seeks to improve students’ English competence in Business and Financial Sectors including English conversation, office English, Business Meetings and Presentations. Extensive Readings in Finance and Economy are required. The objective of this course is to help students familiarize with English often used in the field of Financial sector and business world to enhance their competitiveness in a global world. Intercultural and Financial based knowledge will also be introduced. Basically, this is a project-based course, in which students in a group will have a lot practice and make presentations.

課堂要求：
1. 學生應確實準備所有練習主題，同時需於課餘時間練習；上課時需用心聽講，用心練習，做報告及繳交作業。
2. 學生最多可缺席（包含各類請假，公假及喪假除外）6小時；無故缺席超過6小時者，扣減平常分數。
3. 上課需準時出席，上課時需將手機關機。在上課時間內使用手機上網或講電話、傳簡訊等行為一經發現，平時成績（平均分）即扣一分。
