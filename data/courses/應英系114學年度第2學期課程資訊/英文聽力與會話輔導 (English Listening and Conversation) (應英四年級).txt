課程教學目標：
1. To provide students with up-to-date content, additional grammar practice, and listening and speaking practice to develop learners’ speaking and listening skills through group activities, pair work, role plays, and group discussions.

課程教學綱要：
1. This course will require in-class discussions, oral presentation and role plays for the topics in the textbook. There will be weekly quizzes on vocabulary. Students are encouraged to watch YouTube videos every day and discuss the content with their classmates.
