課程教學目標：
1. Write effective thesis statements and strong supports.
2. Learn writing conventions and structures of academic writing, and adapt to these with creativity.
3. Integrate writing and reading skills acquired prior to this stage of education.
4. Experience various writing processes: brainstorming, prewriting, drafting, revising, editing and finalizing.
5. Write collaboratively and self-expressively.
6. Foster analytical, communication, and presentation skills.

課程教學綱要：
1. This course is designed to build strong connections between writing and reading skills, and adequately prepare students for real-world writing tasks. Students will be required to compose a collection of application documents, such as CV, autobiography, cover letter, etc. Throughout the semester, students will read and analyze sample academic works and other essays of various genres, produce various drafts of the writing assignments, and learn to work collaboratively during the writing process. The ultimate goal of this class is enabling students to utilize writing as a dialogic thinking space, to explore issues of great relevancy, to explore strategic ways of self-representation, and to think far ahead of one’s own career planning.

課堂要求：
Students are expected to attend all the class sessions and attend each class with the assigned readings and homework, and bring with them a single subject, A4-sized notebook specifically for this writing class. Be on time and come prepared to learn and contribute. Late arrival, coming to class empty-handed, or being a phubber in class will lead to a deduction of the final grade. Finish assigned course readings before class; students will be selected at random to answer questions and be graded based on how well they demonstrate understandings of the course reading. Understand and be responsible for your grade.
To facilitate learning outside the classroom, course materials and assignments will be uploaded onto E-campus, an E-learning environment for all NQU courses. All students must register for E-campus in order to gain full access to the updated course information and submit homework online.
