課程教學目標：
1. Experience stages of listening: receiving, understanding, remembering, evaluating, giving feedback
2. Understand vocabulary in context
3. Cultivate the ability to accurately receive and interpret received messages
4. Evaluate arguments and make inferences
5. Develop analytical and critical thinking skills

課程教學綱要：
1. This course aims at helping students enhance listening comprehension in relation to other aspects of academic literacies through in-class discussion and oral presentation.

課堂要求：
Students are expected to attend all class sessions and come prepared with the required course materials. Late arrival, failure to bring course materials, or engaging in distractions such as phone use (“phubbing”) will result in deductions from the final grade. Each group discussion counts toward your participation grade; active engagement is essential. Punctuality is mandatory. Students may be called on at random to answer questions and will be graded based on how well they demonstrate understanding of the assigned readings. There are no make-up quizzes for absences. It is your responsibility to understand the course requirements and manage your performance accordingly. To facilitate learning outside the classroom, course materials and assignments will be uploaded onto E-campus, an E-learning environment for all NQU courses. All students must resister for E-campus and also enter a frequently used email account in order to gain full access to course materials, to receive course updates, to form small groups, and to submit homework online. In an early prevention of an unexpected internet breakdown, please be advised to download weekly reading materials in advance.
