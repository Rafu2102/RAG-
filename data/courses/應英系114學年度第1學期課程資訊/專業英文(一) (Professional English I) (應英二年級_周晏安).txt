課程教學目標：
1. By the end of the first semester, students will be able to: 
- identify the fundamental roles and responsibilities of conference interpreters in professional settings. 
- apply essential skills for consecutive interpreting, including shadowing, active listening, reformulation, and structured note-taking. 
- demonstrate effective memory retention techniques and strategies for managing speech content during interpretation. 
- develop personal glossaries and begin building professional terminology resources based on assigned topics. 
- reflect on individual performance through peer feedback and in-class self-assessment activities 

課程教學綱要：
1. Using Conference Interpreting: A Student’s Practice Book by Andrew Gillies as the core material, students will build a strong foundation in consecutive interpreting during the first semester and develop essential simultaneous interpreting skills in the second semester. Through structured in-class training, real-world simulations, and self-assessment activities, students will gradually acquire the professional competence required for interpreting in international contexts.
