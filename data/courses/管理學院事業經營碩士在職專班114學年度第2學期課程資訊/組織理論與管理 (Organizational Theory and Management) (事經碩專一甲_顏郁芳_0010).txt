課程教學目標：
1. 本課程旨在培養研究生對「組織行為（OB）」核心理論與前沿研究議題之理解與批判思考能力。課程以國際頂尖期刊（如 AMJ、JAP、OBHDP、JOM、PPsych 等）論文為主要教材，透過導讀、討論與批判性文獻回顧，協助學生建構研究問題、理論模型與假說推導，並完成一份可投稿或可延伸為碩論的研究提案。 This course is designed to cultivate graduate students’ theoretical understanding and critical thinking in Organizational Behavior (OB). Using articles from leading journals (e.g., AMJ, JAP, OBHDP, JOM, Personnel Psychology) as core materials, students will engage in guided reading, seminar discussions, and critical literature reviews. The course supports students in developing research questions, theoretical models, and hypothesis development, culminating in a research proposal that can be extended into a thesis or publication.

課程教學綱要：
1. 掌握組織行為核心理論架構（個體、團隊、組織層次）。
2. 閱讀與批判分析頂尖期刊論文之理論、方法與貢獻。
3. 建構具研究價值的研究問題與理論缺口（research gap）。
4. 進行系統化文獻回顧並形成研究命題/假說。
5. 設計可行的研究方法（量化、質化、混合方法）與研究流程。
6. 完成一份高品質研究提案與學術口頭報告。
7. Understand foundational OB theories across individual, team, and organizational levels.
8. Critically evaluate journal articles in terms of theory, methodology, and contribution.
9. Identify meaningful research questions and theoretical gaps.
10. Conduct systematic literature reviews and develop propositions/hypotheses.
11. Design feasible research methodologies (quantitative, qualitative, or mixed).
12. Produce a high-quality research proposal and deliver an academic presentation.
