課程教學目標：
1. Develop skills and qualities of Business public speaking by: --Discovering the importance of speech communication in everyday life --Recognizing the importance of the audience in business speech preparation --Presenting the steps in planning an effective Business speech and Business Proposals and Negotiations --Examining the importance of the parts of a speech: introduction, transition and conclusion --Exploring different techniques in delivery and their effectiveness --Comparing the use of various types of visual aids and their purpose in a speech

課程教學綱要：
1. Activities: Reading/studying of the textbook assignment is required for effective class participation. Class activities will include public speaking, listening, evaluating and constructive critiquing discussions. Midterm and final examinations consist of questions in test format.

成績評定方式：

課堂要求：
Attendance: 2 class absences are permitted without penalty but all assignments are required. Make sure you contact another student for notes, assignments, or hand-outs prior to the next class. Being 10-minutes late for class is regarded as an absence. 
Late Assignments: All assignments are due on the dates announced. Assignments turned in after the due dates will NOT be accepted.
