課程教學目標：
1. This course delves into the ever-changing landscape of contemporary fiction. We'll explore critical issues shaping the genre, trace its historical development, and meet some of the most renowned authors who continue to push its boundaries.

課程教學綱要：
1. Engaging with the Text: Analyze, appreciate, and evaluate assigned novels through class discussions and written assignments.
2. Diversity and Representation: Enhance your understanding of literature by examining different voices and perspectives within the genre.
3. Literary and Social Contexts: Develop awareness of the social and cultural contexts that influence literature and its impact on society.
4. Close Reading and Literary Criticism: Conduct close readings of assigned texts to develop your analytical skills and practice literary criticism.
5. Understanding Readership and Trends: Comprehend the evolution of fiction by studying its target audience, historical trends, and adaptations across media.
