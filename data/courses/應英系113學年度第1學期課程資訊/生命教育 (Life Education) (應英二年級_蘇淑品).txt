課程教學目標：
1. This is basically an English conversation class with a focused on the theme of happiness. There will be independent research, group discussions, presentations and reflective journal entries based on personal action research.

課程教學綱要：
1. Explore different factors involved in defining happiness and understand its function and application in everyday life. Learn how various genetic, social, and economic influences impact your happiness. Recognize how success and achievement impact short versus long-term happiness. Keep a reflective journal as you expand your understanding of happiness, focusing on your life meaning, goals and interests. Write and build your own happiness strategies for your work and life at any age or stage.
