課程教學目標：
1. In this course, you will learn essential principles and methods of classroom motivation, planning and management to prepare you to develop a more effective classroom. You will gain understanding of how to create a learning environment where everyone feels safe, accepted and respected, and learning opportunities are equal. Good classroom management techniques can ensure better and more effective learning outcomes.

課程教學綱要：
1. Gain classroom-management knowledge (terminology, classifications, methods, trends)
2. Learn to apply important principles into your own classes (to improve thinking, problem solving, and decisions)
3. Develop specific classroom management skills, competencies, and points of view needed in the English teaching field.
