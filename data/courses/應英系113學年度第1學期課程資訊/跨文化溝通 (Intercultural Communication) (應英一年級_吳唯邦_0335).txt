課程教學目標：
1. Enhance skills in English readings and writings with informative texts covering a variety of topic, along with active and meaningful discussions in class. It also helps to build up vocabulary with comprehensive questions to help identify main ideas and analyze key concepts. All in all, it aims to develop students’ reading comprehension, vocabulary skills, critical thinking, and to promote cross-cultural communication.

課程教學綱要：
1. To promote English abilities in all aspects; to develop students’ reading comprehension, vocabulary skills, critical thinking, and cross-cultural communication with active and meaningful discussions.
