課程名稱：企業倫理研討 (Business Ethics Seminar)
部別：日間部研究所碩士班
開課班級：企業管理學系碩一
學分數：2.0學分
授課時數：2.0小時
授課教師：蔡亮
必選修：必修
教室：327產業普查研究中心
修課上限人數：30人
上課時間：每周星期四上午第3節~第4節

課程教學目標：
1. 培養學生對於企業倫理議題的敏感度，深入了解倫理內涵與分析角度，進而能夠將其應用在企業決策過程中所面臨的不同倫理困境。企業倫理為規範性知識，但其具有企業發展的策略意涵，可作為工具性知識的應用，更可結合社會企業的發展目標或成為一般企業組織的競爭優勢。 Corporate issues related to ethics of environment, society and governance get more attention as corporations are expected to take more responsibilities In this course, students will learn to nurture the sensibility of ethical issues, to develop the ability of analyzing ethical dilemma, and to build their moral attitude and style in different decision situations. We will also introduce the key theories of corporate ethics and help students ready to start their research on this topic.

課程教學綱要：
1. 了解企業倫理的概念和重要性
2. 了解企業倫理的專業詞彙和不同企業倫理議題
3. 了解企業倫理相關的法規
4. 學習分析企業所面對的社會責任和永續議題
5. 學習將企業倫理與企業經營和發展結合的策略
6. 學習目前國際上企業倫理的最新發展趨勢
7. Understand the concept and importance of business ethics
8. Understand the key concepts of corporate ethics and different corporate ethical issues
9. Understand the laws and regulations related to corporate ethics
10. Learn and analyze social responsibility and sustainable issues faced by enterprises
11. Learn strategies for integrating corporate ethics with business operations and development
12. Learn the latest development trend of international business ethics
