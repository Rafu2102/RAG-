課程教學目標：
1. HRM semiar is designed for:1)graduate students enrolled in survey courses in human resource management who would benefit from a general management approach to strategic HR. 2)working professionals enrolled in specialized HR and executive programs as a capstone offering. and 3)undergraduate students enrolled in a capstone course in an HR degree program or those seeking an advanced level HR course to complement their HRM.

課程教學綱要：
1. The first section examines the context of strategic HRM and develops a framework and conceptual model for the practice of HRM. The second section examines the actual practice and implementation of HRM through a discussion of issues that need to be addressed while developing specific research ideas.
