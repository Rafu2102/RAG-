課程教學目標：
1. To understand the features and importance of medical records.
2. To understand the importance on reading nursing English literature.
3. Familiar with the key points of medical records and nursing English literature in each subject.
4. Able to read the medical records of each department's in hospital.
5. Can use the reading skills of medical records to collect and analyze case health data.
6. Accomplish group presentation for medical records and nursing English literature.

課程教學綱要：
1. This course is taught in English. It is designed to allow students to learn the common terminology of medical records in the hospital systems. Teacher will take the clinical record as an example to provide reading material for students. Students will be familiar with the medical-related English words which are commonly used in clinical field. In order to improve professional literature reading ability, the teacher will also introduce related nursing journals and online resources. By doing so, It is expected that students could have a life-long learning literacy.
2. In order to closely integrate teaching materials and teaching strategies and maximize students' learning efficiency to achieve students' learning goals, the teacher will implement the following teaching strategies:
2-1. Closely integrate teaching materials and teaching strategies in the course preparation process.
2-2. Case teaching method.
2-3. Interrogative learning method.
2-4. Cooperative learning method.
2-5. Diversified teaching strategies and multiple assessments.
