課程教學目標：
1. This course aims to enhance students’ English proficiency by developing their listening, reading, and critical thinking skills through exposure to authentic news articles and podcasts. Students will learn to comprehend main ideas, analyze perspectives, and express informed opinions on current local and global issues.

課程教學綱要：
1. This course is designed to enhance students’ English comprehension and critical thinking skills through guided news reading and podcast listening. Students will explore a wide range of topics from both local and international news sources, particularyly current events. Emphasis will be placed on developing the ability to understand main ideas, recognize supporting details, and evaluate perspectives presented in different media formats. To support diverse learning styles, various visual and audio materials—such as video clips, news broadcasts, and interview excerpts—will be integrated as supplementary resources. These materials aim to enrich students’ understanding of the news content and improve their listening and analytical skills. Assessment will include periodic quizzes, in-class assignments, and reflective tasks designed to track students’ learning progress.
