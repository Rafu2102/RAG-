課程教學目標：
1. This course aims to introduce English speeches that familiarize students with outstanding individuals’ styles and experiences, which will inspire students with independent thinking of confidence of life. The students are also asked to write reflective essays after watching/reading the speeches/scripts, brainstorming, organizing their own ideas and giving feedback to others’ ideas.

課程教學綱要：
1. “The King’s speech” (King George VI’s first wartime radio broadcast)
2. “Grace of Monaco” (Princess Grace of Monaco’s speech for the Red Cross)
3. Ellen DeGeneres’ 2009 commencement speech at Tulane University
4. Steve Jobs’ 2005 Stanford commencement address
5. J.K. Rowling’s 2008 Harvard commencement speech
