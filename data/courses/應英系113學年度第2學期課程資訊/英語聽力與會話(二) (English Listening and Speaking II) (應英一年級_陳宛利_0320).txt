課程教學目標：
1. Experience stages of listening: receiving, understanding, remembering, evaluating, giving feedback
2. Understand vocabulary in context
3. Cultivate the ability to accurately receive and interpret received messages
4. Evaluate arguments and make inferences
5. Develop analytical and critical thinking skills

課程教學綱要：
1. This course aims at helping students enhance listening comprehension in relation to other aspects of academic literacies through in-class discussion and oral presentation.

成績評定方式：
1. 評分比例：出席、課堂參與與簡報、小考 (時間待定) 佔 40%；期中考佔 30%；期末考佔 30%。(Assessment: attendance, class participation & presentation, quiz (TBA)(40%); Midterm Exam (30%); Final Exam (30%))
2. 課堂隨機抽問學生成績：學生將被隨機抽問以回答問題，並根據其展現對閱讀內容理解的程度來進行評分。 (Students will be selected at random to answer questions and be graded based on how well they demonstrate understandings of the reading.)
3. 缺席者不予補考。 (No make-up quizzes for your absences.)
