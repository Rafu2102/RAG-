1. Master Exam-Taking Strategies 2. Enhance Listening Comprehension Skills 3. Build Overall English Proficiency 4. Engage in Practical TOEIC Practice 5. Foster Test Confidence

課程教學綱要：
1. This course is specifically designed to prepare students for the NEW TOEIC test by providing practical exam-taking strategies and tips, including time management and understanding question formats. In addition to these strategies, the course emphasizes building a strong vocabulary and terminology commonly used in the TOEIC test. The class schedule focuses on enhancing listening comprehension skills through targeted exercises and hands-on TOEIC practice sessions. Regular vocabulary-building activities are integrated into the curriculum to support comprehension and improve performance across all sections of the test. Students should also be prepared for occasional pop quizzes and group presentation, which are designed to reinforce learning, assess progress, and ensure retention of key concepts and vocabulary.

課堂要求：
Course Requirements: Students are expected to attend all the class sessions and attend each class with the textbook. Late arrival, coming to class empty-handed, or being a phubber in class will lead to a deduction of the final grade. Be on time and come prepared to learn and contribute. Students will be selected at random to answer questions and be graded based on how well they demonstrate understandings of the reading. No make-up quizzes for your absences. Understand and be responsible for your grade.
To facilitate learning outside the classroom, course materials and assignments will be uploaded onto E-campus and Zuvio, two major E-learning platforms for all NQU courses. All students must register for E-campus and Zuvio, and also enter a frequently used email account in order to gain full access to course materials, to receive course updates, to form small groups, or to submit homework online.
