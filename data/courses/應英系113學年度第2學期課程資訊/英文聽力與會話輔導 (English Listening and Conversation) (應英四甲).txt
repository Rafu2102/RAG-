課程教學目標：
1. This course aims to enhance students' English ability and to develop their skills assessed in the IELTS language test. Students are required to show their ability to use English in a variety of campus and academic situation such as listening to lectures on unfamiliar topics, orally paraphrasing and intergrating information that they have just read and listened to.

課程教學綱要：
1. IElST English reading.
2. IELST English Listening.
3. IELST English writing.
