課程教學目標：
1. This class aims to introduce students to discuss the issues of animals in literature. The topic would include the relationship between animals and human beings, and also how humans reflect the living status of every creature, including their own "beings."

課程教學綱要：
1. how human treat animals
2. what's the differences between human beings and animals
3. animals and women
