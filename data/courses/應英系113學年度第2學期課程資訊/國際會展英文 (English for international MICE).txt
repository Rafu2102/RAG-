課程教學目標：
1. Students will discover and evaluate formal English business terminology, idioms, and expressions relating to conference management and business meeting protocols, in an international environment, by: 1. expanding their vocabulary to include American (and other) expressions commonly used in different aspects of international trade show; 2. analyzing the overview of purpose of international trade show and the complexity of the needs involved; 3. recognizing different problems in conference/ trade show management, developing sound strategies and creating solutions; 4. establishing a variety of entertainment options during free time of the attendees/guests 

課程教學綱要：
1. A discussion of management of the specific challenges relating to tradeshows and exhibitions, including design, construction, and execution. Logistics for planning special events including registration operations, crowd control, security, risk management, speakers, and A/V. Overview of the components of the hospitality market in relation to the Expo/Tradeshow industry. Discussion of current issues facing Conventions/Exhibitions, Vendors/Suppliers, Sponsors, volunteers, and Convention Services Managers. Discussions of current industry trends and related career options.
