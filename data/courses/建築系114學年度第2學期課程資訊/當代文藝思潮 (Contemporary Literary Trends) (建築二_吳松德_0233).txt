課程教學目標：
1. 以歷史發展軸線為主軸，結合藝術史與建築師雙軌，對照重要時間點的相關性，如何相互影響，而產生出的歷史風格以及建築風格的相關性，並且截取重要時間點深入分析與探討所造成的人文藝術與不同區域之間的差異性。 Focusing on the historical development axis, this study combines the dual tracks of art history and architectural history, comparing the relevance of important historical moments, examining how they influence each other, and the correlation between historical and architectural styles that emerged. It also captures key moments for in-depth analysis and exploration of the resulting differences in cultural arts and regional variations.

課程教學綱要：
1. 從文藝復興開始延續到當代，透過重要的主題或人物、事件的介紹與說明一起思考人文藝術與建築的發展，最後拉回到台灣藝術史與建築的重要發展與科技的未來展望。 Starting from the Renaissance and continuing to the present day, this explores the development of humanities, art, and architecture through the introduction and discussion of significant themes, figures, and events. Finally, it brings the focus back to the important developments in Taiwan's art history and architecture, along with future prospects in technology.
