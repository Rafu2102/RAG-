課程教學目標：
1. Enhance skills in English Readings and conversation in various fields with informative texts covering a variety of topic. It seeks to help students build up vocabulary and presentation skills, identify main ideas and analyze key concepts in readings. All in all, it aims to develop students’ reading comprehension, vocabulary skills, critical thinking, and to promote cross-cultural communication 

課程教學綱要：
1. Extensive English readings in various fields as well as English Conversation. Assignments, Group discussions and presentations are crucial to this course.
