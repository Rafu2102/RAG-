課程教學目標：
1. 本課程旨在建立學生對行銷學核心概念與市場導向思維之基礎，涵蓋顧客價值、消費者行為、STP（區隔—目標—定位）、行銷組合（4P/7P）、品牌管理、定價、通路、整合行銷傳播、數位行銷與永續行銷等主題。課程透過案例討論、行銷工具演練與專題提案，使學生能將理論應用於產品/服務設計、競爭策略分析與市場進入規劃，培養行銷分析與企劃能力。 (This course provides students with fundamental marketing concepts and a market-oriented mindset. Key topics include customer value, consumer behavior, STP (segmentation-targeting-positioning), the marketing mix (4Ps/7Ps), branding, pricing, distribution, integrated marketing communications, digital marketing, and sustainable marketing. Through case discussions, marketing tool workshops, and project-based learning, students will apply marketing theories to product/service design, competitive analysis, and marketing planning.)

課程教學綱要：
1. 建立行銷學核心概念與市場導向思維，理解顧客價值與競爭策略。(Develop foundational marketing concepts and a market-oriented mindset, understanding customer value and competitive strategy.)
2. 掌握STP與行銷組合（4P/7P）工具，能進行行銷策略規劃與執行設計。(Master STP and the marketing mix (4Ps/7Ps) to design and implement marketing strategies.)
3. 理解消費者行為與顧客旅程，能分析顧客需求並提出市場解決方案。(Understand consumer behavior and the customer journey, analyze customer needs, and propose market solutions.)
4. 能分析市場與競爭情勢（如SWOT、五力分析），提出定位與差異化策略。(Analyze markets and competition (e.g., SWOT, Porter’s Five Forces) and propose positioning and differentiation strategies.)
5. 具備品牌、定價、通路與整合行銷傳播之基礎管理能力。(Build fundamental management capabilities in branding, pricing, distribution, and integrated marketing communications.)
6. 培養行銷倫理、永續與數位行銷素養，能因應新興市場與科技趨勢。(Cultivate marketing ethics, sustainability, and digital marketing literacy to respond to emerging markets and technology trends.)
