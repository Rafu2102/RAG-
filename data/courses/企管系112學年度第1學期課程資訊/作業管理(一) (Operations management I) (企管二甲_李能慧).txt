課程教學目標：
1. 本課程著重於理論與個案並重方式，以策略導向及價值鏈觀點，說明生產與作業管理的內涵及其在企業組織中的重要性。探討主題包括作業策略、作業系統選擇、作業系統設計、作業系統管制及供應鏈規劃設計等，並輔以國內外產業個案及生活實例，使學生了解企業生產與作業層面管理功能的運用，從中獲知企業作業管理的效益。預期學生可獲得以下核心能力：1.全球化觀點；2.分析與決策能力；3.管理知識與執行力；4.溝通與協調能力；5.企業倫理觀與同理心。 This course focuses on both theory and cases, and explains the connotation of production and operation management and its importance in enterprise organization from the perspective of strategy orientation and value chain. Discussion topics include operation strategy, operation system selection, operation system design, operation system control and supply chain planning and design, supplemented by domestic and foreign industrial cases and life examples, so that students can understand the application of enterprise production and operation level management functions, and learn from them The benefits of business operations management. Students are expected to acquire the following core abilities: 1. Global perspective; 2. Analysis and decision-making ability; 3. Management knowledge and execution ability; 4. Communication and coordination ability; 5. Corporate ethics and empathy.

課程教學綱要：
1. 本課程共分為十八章，主要介紹作業管理領域的相關概念，包含：作業管理導論、作業管理規劃、作業管理執行工具及相關的概念。 This course is divided into 18 chapters, mainly introducing related concepts in the field of job management, including: introduction to job management, job management planning, job management execution tools and related concepts.
