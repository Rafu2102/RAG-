課程教學目標：
1. This course examines the social, economic, cultural, political and environmental dimensions of sustainability and sustainable development for enterprises. Specific areas of focus include indicators of definition of sustainability, environmental friendly design and manufacture, industry sustainability, sustainable systems. To better understand these concepts in a real world context, students will implement projects related to sustainable development for industry partners. The course will be half lecture and discussion and half workshop on projects with industry partners. Throughout, students will confront the challenges of implementing their ideas in the real world. 透過修習本課程，學生可培養本系之核心能力，課程內容的核心能力佔比如下，全球化觀點10%、分析與決策能力30%、管理知識與執行力40%、倫理觀與同理心20%。

課程教學綱要：
1. During this class, you will:
(1) Understand the various aspects of sustainable industries;
(2) Learn about tools for measuring sustainability;
(3) Strengthen your ability to work in a team to address a sustainability issue; and,
(4) Create a professional quality product related to sustainability that an industry partner can actually use.

成績評定方式：
1. Final evaluations will reflect the quality of class participation and weekly memos (30%), mid-term exam (30%), final exam (40%). We will assess the quality of students’ work based on its substance (depth, originality) and presentation (clarity, effectiveness).

課堂要求：
Attendance at class and out-of-class team meetings is mandatory. In addition to preparing a final deliverable, student teams will make in-class presentations and a final presentation to the enterprise partner.
