課程教學目標：
1. The notion of pop culture is not easily delineated. It is a multifaceted and intricate concept encompassing many phenomena, including fashion, chic, consumer culture, leisure culture, luxury culture, material culture, popular lifestyles, popular tastes, urban culture, subculture, mass culture, and mass culture. This expansive concept denotes culture disseminated across various levels, classes, and population strata in a specific region or globally, following a certain pace and cycle. This course aims to facilitate an exploration and understanding of the intricate relationship between modern popular culture and capitalism. Through this exploration, students will develop a post-critical reflective perspective while engaging with the subject matter dynamically and engagingly.

課程教學綱要：
1. This course's objective is to facilitate an exploration and comprehension of the intertwined dynamics between modern popular culture, capitalism, and globalization. It is designed to foster the development of a critical and reflective perspective while also ensuring an engaging and enjoyable learning experience.
