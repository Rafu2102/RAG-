課程教學目標：
1. 中文：1.訓練具有建築修養的設計者及具獨立思考分析能力的設計研究人員。 2.培養環境規畫領域所需之實務、教學研究人才，強調理論與實戰結合。 3.培養跨領域建築理論之專業研究基礎人員。 English：1.Training architects and design researchers with independent thinking and analytical ability. 2.Training in the field of environmental planning required for teaching and research personnel, and stressed that the combination of theory and practical. 3.Cultivate interdisciplinary architectural theory the professional research foundation staff.

課程教學綱要：
1. 中文：傳授學生進階知識與最新理論、技術，養成學生獨立研究及應用知識之能力，在課堂上互動研討，及時解答問題。 English：Teach students advanced knowledge and the latest theories, technology, develop students' independent research and the ability to apply knowledge, Interactive workshops in the classroom, to answer questions in a timely manner.
