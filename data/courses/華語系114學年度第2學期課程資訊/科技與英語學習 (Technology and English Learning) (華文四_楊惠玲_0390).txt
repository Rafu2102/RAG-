課程教學目標：
1. The arrangement of this course aims to enhance students' literacy and proficiency in foreign languages, helping them grasp the trends of future society through the latest technology. Thus, this course integrates modern technology and AI tools into the process of English language learning, focusing on enhancing students' linguistic proficiency and technical skills. Through a structured weekly curriculum, students will explore various AI applications and tools that support their language acquisition, creative expressions, and effective communications.

課程教學綱要：
1. Students will progressively work with practical tools like text and speech generators, image-to-text software, and AI music generators. These technologies will enable students to improve their English language abilities while fostering creativity and critical thinking.
2. Hands-on activities such as creating lesson plans, using language assessment tools, and participating in group presentations will prepare students for real-world applications of their skills.
3. The course also includes interactive outings that blend social engagement with creative writing, allowing students to collaborate and enhance their communication and teamwork skills. By the end of the course, students will have developed a strong foundation in both technology use and English communication, equipped with the knowledge and tools to apply their learning beyond the classroom.
