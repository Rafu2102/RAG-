課程教學目標：
1. In this course, we will exercise the four language skills, including listening, speaking, reading, and writing. Additionally, we will focus on training daily life conversation in order to equip with the fundamental communication ability. Immersing in the authentic learning environment, we will grant a great opportunity to practice English actively & joyfully.
