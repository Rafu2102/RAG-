課程教學目標：
1. a) Experience stages of listening: receiving, understanding, remembering, evaluating, giving feedback b) Understand vocabulary in context c) Cultivate the ability to accurately receive and interpret received messages d) Evaluate arguments and make inferences e) Develop analytical and critical thinking skills

課程教學綱要：
1. This course aims at helping students enhance listening comprehension in relation to other aspects of academic literacies through in-class discussion and oral presentation.

課堂要求：
Students are expected to attend all the class sessions and attend each class with the textbook. Late arrival, coming to class empty-handed, or being a phubber in class will lead to a deduction of the final grade. Be on time and come prepared to learn and contribute. Students will be selected at random to answer questions and be graded based on how well they demonstrate understandings of the reading. No make-up quizzes for your absences. Understand and be responsible for your grade.
To facilitate learning outside the classroom, course materials and assignments will be uploaded onto E-campus, an E-learning environment for all NQU courses. All students must register for E-campus and also enter a frequently used email account in order to gain full access to course materials, to receive course updates, to form small groups, and to submit homework online. In an early prevention of an unexpected internet breakdown, please be advised to download weekly reading materials in advance. For course-related questions, contact me at tmpmlily@gmail.com.
