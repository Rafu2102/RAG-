課程教學目標：
1. Course Description: This course cultivates students’ writing ability by promoting their knowledge of the genres particular to English academic writing. A strong emphasis is based on heightening students’ awareness of genre conventions by having students read in abundance and then write meaningful paragraphs and essays. The first semester focuses on paragraphs and second semester focuses on both paragraphs and essays.

課程教學綱要：
1. Course Objectives: Students will build and develop writing skills by:
1. Analyzing and applying the steps of the writing process;
2. Examining the purpose for, and the steps in writing different types of essays;
3. Pointing out the importance of grammar, with successful application of skills and principles.
4. Applying editing techniques to personal and selected writings.

Attendance:
One class or 2 hours of absence will be permitted without penalty; 2 points will be subtracted from your FINAL GRADE for every additional hour of absence. If you are more than 10 minutes late, you are counted as absent for that hour. If your unexcused absences are more than 5 days (or 10 hours) you Fail the class.
