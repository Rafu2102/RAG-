課程教學目標：
1. This class offers students the opportunities to apply what they learn in school to actual business settings. Through the internship, students will to get to know more about the practical business world, acquiring knowledge and related skills, such as how to deal with customers and so on. Along with feedbacks from their employers, this project would be helpful for students’ future career planning.

課程教學綱要：
1. Students practice the knowledge of business at business settings.
