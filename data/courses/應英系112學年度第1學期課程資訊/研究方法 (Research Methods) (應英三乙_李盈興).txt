課程教學目標：
1. This course aims to provide students with basic concepts and techniques in doing empirical research. Students will learn how they can ask specific questions, link their questions to applicable methods, and interpret their data appropriately. Students will have hands-on experiences in conducting attitude survey and experimental study on the topics that they choose. The procedure from literature review, survey design, data analysis to research writing and presentation will be guided in a step-by-step manner. Students will also learn to edit their data and plot the graphs using Microsoft Excel.

課程教學綱要：
1. The content of this semester contains:
- Making a logical proposal
- Finding resources and reviewing previous studies
- Designing a survey and an experiment
- Data collection, analysis, and graphing
- Constructing a formal Microsoft PowerPoint file
- Presenting research results

成績評定方式：
1. Presentations: 30% * 2 (共計 60%)
2. Assignments: 30%
3. Participation: 10%

課堂要求：
Students of every group should construct two research projects and complete two oral presentations for this course. The teacher will check attendance on all the meeting occasions. Students should participate in the group discussion after classes in order to prepare the requirements of the course every time. Students should respect the others who are presenting their research in class by not talking aloud, wandering from the seats or surfing the Internet on their smartphones.
