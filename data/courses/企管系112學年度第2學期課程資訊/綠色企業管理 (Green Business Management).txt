課程教學目標：
1. (1)Introduce the concept of Inclusive and Sustainable Industrial Development and its relation with environmental matters. (2)Reflect on the necessary parameters of governance systems (strategies, policies, laws and regulations) for green industrial development. Achieving low carbon and efficient material use paths for industrial development is a key aspect. (3)Outline practical feasibility of technical requirements and policy measures for promoting green industry. (4)Discuss how enterprises can successfully tackle key challenges that prevent them from commercializing Green Industry-related products and services. (5)Delineate the essential institutional platforms for promoting “Green Industry”. Apart from promoting resource efficient and cleaner production, such institutions should offer counsel on the development and deployment of economically viable, socially appropriate and environmentally sustainable green technologies. (6)Enable participants to construct and make use of Green Industry strategies that help meet specific future environmental goals and targets.

課程教學綱要：

3. PART III. POLICIES FOR SUPPORTING GREEN INDUSTRY
4. PART IV. CASE STUDYING

成績評定方式：
1. 評量比例與方式：課堂參與和每週備忘錄 (class participation and weekly memos) 佔 30%、考試 (exam) 佔 30%、專案報告 (project work) 佔 30%、同儕互評 (students’ peer evaluations) 佔 10%。
2. 評分標準：學生成就將基於內容深度與原創性 (substance: depth, originality) 以及簡報清晰度與有效性 (presentation: clarity, effectiveness) 進行評估。

課堂要求：
課堂出席以及課後的團隊會議為強制性要求。學生團隊除了準備最終成果（final deliverable）外，還需進行課堂報告（in-class presentations），並在可能的情況下向合作企業進行最終報告（final presentation to the enterprise partner）。
