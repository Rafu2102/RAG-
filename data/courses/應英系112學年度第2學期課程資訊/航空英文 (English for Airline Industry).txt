課程教學目標：
1. In order to prepare students to become the ground crews or flight attendants for their future career, students will be able to: 1) know the English terms, abbreviations, city codes, carrier codes of countries all over the world. 2) be familiar with the English vocabularies, expressions, or talks on the ground or in flights with the travelers from different countries. 3) be well-prepared for pre-interviewing in English autobiography and resume for their careers as ground crews or flight attendants through job analysis and written exam.

課程教學綱要：
1. The theme-based contents in this course for working preparations as ground crews or flight attendants will be listed as below:
1. English terms, abbreviations, city codes, carrier codes of countries in aviation career.
2) English vocabularies, expressions, announcements, talks and dialogues used for flight attendants/ground crews.
3) The English formats of autobiography and resume as ground crews/flight attendants and job interviewing in English.
