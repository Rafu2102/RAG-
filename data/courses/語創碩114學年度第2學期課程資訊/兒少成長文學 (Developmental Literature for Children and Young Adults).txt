課程教學目標：
1. Reading is magic: a physical act of focused absorption. For children and emerging young adults, reading activates the cognitive imagination. To learn how to read is an act of labor and devotion. Books offer the possibility of an aesthetic life through imaginative training. The core benefit of reading is for the young to experience the best qualities in life, as represented by the writers. Let’s plunge into the world of imagination conveyed by children’s and young readers’ books. 

課程教學綱要：
1. Analyze, appreciate, and evaluate the weekly readings across different developmental stages.
2. Enhance understanding and address issues of diversity in children's and young adult literature.
3. Develop awareness of issues associated with literature addressing childhood and emerging adulthood.
4. Comprehend literature for these age groups by studying its readership, its trends, and its adaptations into other media.

成績評定方式：
1. Class Participation, Discussion, and Presentation: 60%
2. Term Paper: 40%

課堂要求：
*Active Learning is Crucial: You must come prepared to speak and present ideas regularly. 
*Balance of Skills: Balances oral/active learning with traditional academic writing. 
*Consistent Effort: Consistent effort throughout the semester (showing up prepared, contributing) will be more rewarding than cramming for a single final exam or paper. 
*Synthesis of Knowledge: The presentations and discussions will require you to synthesize weekly readings and engage with course concepts on an ongoing basis, feeding into the more focused analysis required for the term paper.
