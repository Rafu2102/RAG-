課程教學目標：
1. This course is intended to familiarize students with in a wide variety of English reading and listening contexts and help them develop English proficiency from target vocabulary and structures that they already know without undue struggling effort. Students are strongly encouraged to participate in weekly vocabulary quizzes and listening/reading comprehension exercises in the classroom. The topics of course cover sociology, technology, geography, history, economics, literature, space, and people. The vocabulary for this course is organized on the mnemonic function of everyday schemata that makes students in preparation for advanced English usage.
